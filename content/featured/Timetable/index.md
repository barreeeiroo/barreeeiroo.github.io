My final thesis was about programmatically solving the Curriculum-Based Course Timetabling problem (_CB-CTT_) and
implementing a solution for my university. As part of its research, I developed a full platform to manage all
schedules and timetables for any university.

It is a fully serverless platform hosted on AWS, and it provides all flexibility any university may need for their
organizational structure. The open-source part of the project is the solving algorithm using Answer-Set Programming
(_ASP_) with Clingo.
